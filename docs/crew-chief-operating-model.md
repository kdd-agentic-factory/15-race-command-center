# Crew Chief Operating Model

The crew chief console turns model output into controlled engineering action.

It manages:

- Geometry changes.
- Engine maps.
- Traction control.
- Anti-wheelie.
- Engine braking.
- Brake balance.
- Suspension preload, compression and rebound.
- Ride height, wheelbase and swingarm settings.
- Final drive.
- Tire pressures and compounds.
- Aero and circuit-specific parts.

Every recommendation includes evidence, expected impact, risk and approval state.
